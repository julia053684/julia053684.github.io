Julia Lewis MSc

I am currently Studying in my final year of a MSc in Clinical Hypnotherapy at the University of West London, already holding a PGDip in Clinical Hypnotherapy.

I am also a supervisor for the London College of Clinical Hypnosis. Supporting others as discover the benefits that hypnosis has to offer.

I trained in clinical hypnotherapy because I wanted to find out it’s benefits both for myself and others.

I utilise hypnosis techniques in combination with Positive Psychology Principles to help clients to “step back” and gain insight into their issues, allowing them to generate options and strategies to resolve these.

Positive Psychology advocates that we can all benefit from focussing on positive emotions and developing our own unique individual strengths.

I offer a professional and welcoming clinical hypnotherapy and coaching service where we can work together, at an agreed pace, to address the goals that are important to you.
