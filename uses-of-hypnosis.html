Clinical uses

{% include page-image.html image='shutterstock_149669174' %}

Hypnosis is applied in a clinical setting for many purposes, including...

  • Pain relief
  • Overcoming phobia
  • Changing unwanted habits and behaviours
  • Managing depression, stress and anxiety conditions
  • Addressing emotional issues such as anger management, grief and loss


Self development

{% include page-image.html image='shutterstock_168877688' %}

Hypnosis is also used for the purpose of developing and enhancing general well-being:

  • Motivation
  • Goal setting
  • Confidence building and developing appropriate assertive behaviour
  • Self-awareness and personal development
  • Relaxation
